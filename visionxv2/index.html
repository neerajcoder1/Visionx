About Vision X

A team built on
Innovation.

Vision X is a multidisciplinary technology team formed by passionate students committed to solving real-world problems through innovation and technology.

We actively participate in hackathons, internships, startup initiatives, and collaborative projects to transform ideas into impactful digital products across AI, Web Development, Blockchain, and Sustainability.

Our mission is to become a leading student innovation team building impactful products and empowering future technology leaders.
